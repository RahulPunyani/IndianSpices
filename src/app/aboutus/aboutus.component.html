About us!

Jaiswal Impex was established in October 1986 as an Importer-Exporter and providing consultancy services to get the benefits from Govt. of India. Currently we are exporting superior quality of Indian spices and Basmati Rice in the International market.

Our Spices are processed by First Cryogenic Grinding Technology for producing inimitable range of quality spices. All the food products are certified by various food regulators such as FSSAI, HACCP and is ISO - 9001-2008 certified too.

The Success Mantra for us is by providing product which is value for money and fulfil our customer’s expectation with high standards. We have our overseas office in Sofia, Bulgaria under the name of Jaiswal Impex OOD.

Our organisation provide high standard of goods and services to our clients. And we have many satisfied clients around the world and in India. We are the Member of EPCH – Export Promotion Council of Handicrafts, MSME- Micro, Small, Medium Enterprise from Ministry of Commerce India and Chamber of Commerce, Bulgaria.
